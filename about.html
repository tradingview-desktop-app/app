About Trade View

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Trade View emerged from recognizing the need for accessible market intelligence tools. Our platform combines AI technology with comprehensive data analysis to support independent market research.

What began as a project to enhance market analysis capabilities has evolved into a platform that serves researchers and analysts worldwide, providing tools for thorough market examination.

Our Mission

At Trade View, we aim to provide accessible tools for market analysis and research. We believe in empowering users with information to support their independent decision-making process.

Our focus includes:

  • Making market analytics tools accessible
  • Supporting independent research capabilities
  • Enhancing market data transparency
  • Advancing AI applications in market analysis
  • Facilitating informed research processes
